## 1. PLATFORM OVERVIEW

Nairobi Verified is a comprehensive business verification and marketplace platform that serves as a trusted intermediary between businesses and customers in Nairobi, Kenya. The platform addresses the critical challenge of business authenticity in the digital age by providing a rigorous verification process that establishes trust and credibility for legitimate businesses.

### 1.1 Primary Purpose

The platform serves three main purposes:
1. **Business Authentication**: Verify the legitimacy and credibility of businesses through document verification and physical validation
2. **Digital Marketplace**: Provide verified businesses with professional online storefronts and e-commerce capabilities
3. **Customer Protection**: Offer customers a trusted directory of verified businesses, reducing fraud and enhancing shopping confidence

### 1.2 Target Users

**Primary Users:**
- **Businesses**: Small to medium enterprises seeking credibility and online presence
- **Customers**: Individuals looking for trusted businesses and services
- **Administrators**: Nairobi Verified team managing verifications and platform operations

**Secondary Users:**
- **Partners**: Business service providers and stakeholders
- **Investors**: Parties interested in platform growth and analytics
- **Regulators**: Government bodies monitoring business activities

**Registration Process:**
1. **Initial Registration**
   - Business owners create accounts with basic information
   - Email verification and phone number confirmation
   - Password creation with security requirements
   - Terms of service acceptance

2. **Business Profile Creation**
   - Business name and description
   - Category selection (Restaurant, Retail, Services, etc.)
   - Contact information and location details
   - Business hours and operating information

3. **Document Submission**
   - Business Registration Certificate upload
   - Owner identification documents (National ID/Passport)

## 4. USER WORKFLOWS AND PROCESSES

### 4.1 Business Onboarding Workflow

**Step 1: Registration**
- Business owner accesses registration form
- Provides basic business and contact information
- Creates secure account with email verification
- Accepts platform terms and conditions

**Step 2: Profile Setup**
- Completes comprehensive business profile
- Uploads business logo and photos
- Configures business hours and contact methods
- Sets up initial product categories

**Step 3: Document Verification**
- Uploads required verification documents
- System performs initial document validation
- Administrative team reviews submissions
- Additional documentation requested if needed

**Step 4: Verification Review**
- Manual review by verification specialists
- Document authenticity confirmation
- Business legitimacy verification
- Physical location verification (if required)

**Step 5: Approval and Activation**
- Verification status update and notification
- Public profile activation
- Access to full merchant features
- Welcome package and training materials

### 4.2 Customer Shopping Workflow

**Step 1: Business Discovery**
- Customer searches or browses businesses
- Filters results by category, location, ratings
- Views business profiles and verification status
- Reads reviews and compares options

**Step 2: Product Selection**
- Browses business product catalogs
- Views detailed product information
- Compares prices and specifications
- Adds items to cart or wishlist

**Step 3: Purchase Process**
- Reviews cart items and quantities
- Provides delivery or pickup information
- Selects payment method
- Completes secure checkout

**Step 4: Order Management**
- Receives order confirmation
- Tracks order status updates
- Communicates with merchant if needed
- Receives product and confirms delivery

**Step 5: Post-Purchase**
- Leaves review and rating
- Provides feedback on experience
- Saves merchant to favorites
- Shares recommendations with others

### 4.3 Administrative Verification Workflow

**Step 1: Application Review**
- New verification applications appear in queue
- Administrator assigns reviewer
- Initial document completeness check
- Priority assignment based on business type

**Step 2: Document Analysis**
- Detailed review of submitted documents
- Cross-reference with official databases
- Verification of business registration details
- Authentication of identification documents

**Step 3: Background Verification**
- Business legitimacy verification
- Owner background check
- Location and contact verification
- Reference checks (if applicable)

**Step 4: Decision Making**
- Verification approval or rejection decision
- Detailed reasoning documentation
- Communication with business owner
- Status update in system

**Step 5: Post-Verification Management**
- Ongoing monitoring of verified businesses
- Periodic reverification processes
- Complaint and dispute resolution
- Performance and compliance tracking
